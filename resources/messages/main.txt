Hello! This bot combines the convenience of Telegram, the power of ChatGPT, and the fun of Tinder.


*The chatbot can:*

- Generate a Tinder profile based on a description 😎
- Write interesting and intriguing messages for starting a conversation 😘
- Chat on your behalf 🔥
- You can practice chatting with the chatbot 🥰


*Useful commands and links:*

/start - main menu of the bot
/profile - generate a Tinder profile 😎
/opener - opening message for dating 🥰
/message - chat on your behalf 😈
/date - chat with celebrities 🔥
/gpt - ask a question to ChatGPT 🧠
